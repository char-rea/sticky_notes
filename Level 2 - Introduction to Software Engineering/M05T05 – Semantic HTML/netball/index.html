Introduction to Netball

Netball is a fast-paced team sport that is popular in many countries around the world.

History of Netball

Netball was invented in the late 19th century as a variation of basketball, specifically designed for women.

Rules of Netball

Netball is played with two teams of seven players each.

Netball Positions

Goal Shooter, Goal Attack, Wing Attack, Centre, Wing Defence, Goal Defence, Goal Keeper.

Netball Competitions

Netball is played at various levels.

The Netball World Cup and Commonwealth Games are major events.

Benefits of Playing Netball

Netball improves fitness, teamwork, and coordination.

Netball Equipment

Players need a netball, hoop, and suitable footwear.

Netball Training and Skills

Training focuses on passing, shooting, and fitness.

Netball Culture and Community

Netball has a strong, inclusive community.

Netball and Gender Equality

Netball promotes women's participation in sport.
